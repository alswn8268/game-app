■ 자세한 설명 (English — 영어 버전도 등록 권장)
----------------------------------------------
⭕ Simple. Addictive. Impossible to put down.

A ring shrinks toward the center.
Tap at the perfect moment — score big.
Miss it — lose a life.

That's all. So why can't you stop playing?

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🎯 How to Play
• A ring shrinks from the outside in
• Tap when the ring enters the GREEN zone → GOOD
• Wait for the WHITE zone → PERFECT (2× score!)
• Miss the timing → lose 1 life
• 3 lives gone = Game Over

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

⚡ Features
• COMBO system — up to 3× score multiplier
• Progressive difficulty — gets faster every level
• Personal best tracking
• One-handed mobile gameplay
• Watch ad to continue
